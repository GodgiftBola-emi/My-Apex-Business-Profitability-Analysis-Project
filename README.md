Introduction:
Purpose:
In today’s data-centric business landscape, organizations that effectively leverage data gain a significant competitive advantage. This project focuses on transforming raw sales data into actionable insights that can inform strategic decisions and improve overall business performance.

Objective of the Project:
The objective of this analysis is to evaluate sales performance across time, geography, product categories, and sales personnel, using Microsoft Excel to uncover patterns that influence revenue generation and business growth.
Problem Being Addressed
Despite generating a total revenue of $435,066, there is limited clarity on:

Why certain regions, particularly high-performing cities, outperform others
Which product categories contribute most significantly to revenue
How individual salespeople influence performance outcomes
What drives seasonal spikes, especially the sharp increase observed in December
This analysis aims to bridge these gaps by providing a structured and data-driven breakdown of business performance.
